\noindent
\textbf{Manuscript:} ``Institutional fragility and climatic exposure drive biocultural vulnerability in traditional agricultural knowledge systems''

\vspace{12pt}

\noindent
This study is a systematic review and hierarchical meta-analysis based exclusively on previously published, publicly available literature. No primary data collection involving human participants, animal subjects, or biological samples was conducted. Accordingly, no ethical approval or informed consent was required.

\vspace{12pt}

\noindent
All data sources are fully cited and the complete extraction dataset is deposited on Mendeley Data (DOI: 10.17632/mh5sbx7ky7.1). The systematic review protocol is registered on the Open Science Framework (DOI: 10.17605/OSF.IO/HE8KT).
